# Collegopedia
Collegopedia helps students to find their choice of colleges by making use of various filter criteria such as fee structure, 
location of colleges,college hostel facilities,etc. The students will get list of colleges based on vaious filter criteria. 
This will save crucial time and efforts of students.

Collegopedia is also useful for those colleges who are new to the educational industry. This will help them to save their 
advertisement cost and also help them circulate their college detail among students.
